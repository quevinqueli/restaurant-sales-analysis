## Project Overview

This project analyzes point-of-sale transaction data from a steakhouse restaurant to identify key revenue drivers, peak performance periods, and actionable business recommendations.

The goal is to help restaurant owners make data-driven decisions to increase revenue, optimize staffing, and improve overall business performance.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Business Questions Answered

Which day of the week generates the most revenue?
What are the top 10 best selling items by revenue?
Which hours are the busiest by day?
Which server generates the most revenue?
What is the revenue split between order types?

Some of these questions can vary according to clients needs. :-)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Key Findings

Saturday, Friday and Sunday are the top 3 revenue days. Thursday is the weakest.
New York Strip, Filet Mignon and Ribeye Steak drive the most revenue.
Evening slots 19:00-22:30 dominate across all days and Saturday 21:30 is the single most profitable slot.
Nina is the top performing server at €91,507 — a €7,942 gap separates best from worst.
Delivery is the strongest order channel at €151,637 total revenue

## Business Impact

These insights enable my clients to:

Optimise staffing levels on peak days and hours
